Eastern Ghats Mobile Belt

Geological Field Study – Bikrant Kumar Mishra

About Project

This study documents geological structures and metamorphic rocks from the Eastern Ghats Mobile Belt in Odisha. Observed structures include folds, faults, gneissic banding, dyke intrusions and garnetiferous metamorphic rocks.
